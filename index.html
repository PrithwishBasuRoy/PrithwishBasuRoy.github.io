About Prithwish

Prithwish Basu Roy is currently a research scholar at the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology, Madras. He is currently pursuing his Master of Science(by Research) degree under the guidance of Dr. Chester Rebeiro. Prithwish's research interests lies in various topics of Hardware Security like Fault Injection Attacks and it's Defences, Power Side-Channel Analysis, Hardware Trojan Detection and Firmware Reverse Engineering. His paper Avatar: Reinforcing Fault Attack Countermeasures in EDA with Fault Transformations has been accepted in ASP-DAC 2022. He also won the first prize in the CSWAW'21 Embedded Security Challenge, India Region.
Prior to joining IIT Madras as research scholar Prithwish worked as Senior Systems Engineer for Infosys.
